SYS.01 / Crew Manifest

Why Hire Me?

The Case for Eshaana
▸ Dual degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Planetary Science from Purdue. In five years. I said what I said.
▸ I build things that work — from flexible PCBs for surgical devices to satellite image processing pipelines. Range is a feature, not a bug.
▸ I grew up watching Chandrayaan missions from India. Now I write research papers about them. Full circle.
▸ I can read a CAD file and a spectral dataset in the same afternoon and make them talk to each other.

Growing up in India, I watched the Chandrayaan missions with the kind of obsession most kids reserved for cartoons. That curiosity turned into a Planetary Science degree. The engineering degree came because I realised I didn't just want to understand space — I wanted to build things for it.

Purdue gave me both, in five chaotic, caffeinated years. Along the way I analysed Martian craters, designed a surgical infection-monitoring device that made it to the finals of an Innovation Award, and grew food in a Mars Desert Research Station simulation. Standard stuff.

I'm now looking for work that sits at the intersection of engineering, science, and making things that actually go somewhere — literally or figuratively.

SYS.02 / Flight Systems

The Work

Operation 01 · Remote Sensing
Mars & Moon · Remote Sensing Studies
Band stretching, false colour composites, radar reflectivity, and elevation mapping across Mars and the Moon. Processing raw satellite data into something that actually tells you something — clay minerals, calderas, water.
Chandrayaan 1 & M3 GRAIL & LOLA Viking Lander Dragonfly · Titan Martian Calderas Water on the Moon
Operation 02 · Biomedical
Surgical Site Infection Monitor
Senior design project with Zimmer Biomet. Built a wearable device that monitors biomarkers to detect post-surgical infections before they become dangerous. Responsible for the flexible PCB design, electronics, and the web-based GUI. Made it to the Innovation Award finals. Third place, People's Choice.
Images coming soon
Operation 03 · Mission Proposals
Deep Space Mission Design
Formal mission proposals for Callisto (BEAR Mission) and a low-Earth orbit study. The kind of documents that start with "we propose to land on..." and end with a budget.
BEAR Mission · Callisto OCD in LEO
Operation 04 · Field Work
Agrivoltaics & MDRS Simulation
Grew food at a Mars Desert Research Station simulation. Yes, that's a real thing. Also worked on agrivoltaic systems — farming under solar panels, because if we're going to live on Mars we'll need to eat something.
Files uploading soon
Operation 05 · Design & Fabrication
CAD & 3D Printing
Everything from instrument housing designs to prototypes that needed to survive multiple iterations and not fall apart. SolidWorks, 3D printing, and the occasional moment of "this actually works."
Portfolio uploading soon
Operation 06 · This Website
You're Looking at It
Built from scratch with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Mission control aesthetic, animated starfield, real-time clock, CRT typewriter intro, and a radar that goes round and round for no functional reason. Worth it.
